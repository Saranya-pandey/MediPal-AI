# 🏥 MediPal AI

**An AI-Powered Personal Health Management System**

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Managing healthcare today is highly fragmented and inefficient.

Prescriptions are often misplaced, medicine schedules are forgotten, and expiry dates go unnoticed. Medical reports remain scattered across different platforms, hospitals, and devices, making it difficult to access critical information when needed.

For families, the problem is even worse — there is no simple way to manage multiple members’ health data in one place.

👉 This results in missed medications, delayed treatments, and poor health management.

---

## 💡 Solution

**MediPal AI** is a centralized digital health platform that enables users to manage their medicines, prescriptions, and medical records seamlessly in one place.

It acts as a **personal health assistant + digital health locker**, ensuring that users never lose track of their medical information.

---

## 🎯 Use Cases

### 👤 Individual Users

* Track daily medications
* Receive timely reminders
* Store and access prescriptions anytime

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Families

* Manage health records of multiple members
* Maintain a shared medical history
* Organize prescriptions and reports for parents/children

### 🚑 Emergency Situations

* Instantly access critical medical data
* Share reports quickly with doctors
* Avoid delays in treatment
